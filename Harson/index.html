Introduction

AN ANSWER TO THE PAPER INDUSTRY NEEDS.

PT. Harson Multiline Machinery was established to answer the paper industry’s need for dependable, precision-built components. We specialize in high-grade industrial, custom roller manufacturing, and precision repair services—including specialized and newly engineered spreader rolls—each built with careful craftsmanship and measured accuracy. Every component is treated as a critical moving part—balanced, aligned, and refined to ensure smooth rotation, consistent tension, and long-term reliability on the production line.
